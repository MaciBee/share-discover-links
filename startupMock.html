Startup Founder Advice

Paul Graham

Listen to what people are saying

You can't just accept what is on the surface. Understand the context. And try to understand why people think that something is a good idea. And how their situation is similar or different than yours.

Start small and evolve

It's essential to begin with a minimal viable product and gradually refine it based on user feedback and market demands.

Finding enthusiastic users

Try to find at least one person who cares, one person who is enthusiastic about what you are doing. These early adopters can provide valuable insights and feedback.
